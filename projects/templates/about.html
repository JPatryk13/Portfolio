About Me

Software engineer and website full-stack developer, currently finishing a degree in Engineering Physics. I am currently based in Aberystwyth, a cosy town in Wales.

My motivation is within the process of creation, the possibility of learning and merging skills I have gained. Recently, I have initiated a web-dev project, which is an online store with paintings for amateur and semi-professional artists, which I hope to develop and expand on in the future. This, as well as the portfolio website you are browsing right now, were made using Django framework which I hope to utilise further.


My adventure with programming and web-dev started around 6 years ago, learning C++ and the basics of front-end web development (HTML and CSS). Then, 3 years later I learnt Python and shortly after that I learnt PHP and Laravel framework. Since then I moved back to Python language and introduced myself to Django.

For the past three years, I have also learnt C Arduino, MATLAB, JavaScript, basics of website design and, last but not least, how to manage my time so I can fit all these into my schedule.

Most of my experience can be proven in the here. If it seems interesting to you, feel free to I will be happy to hear what you think about my recent work.
